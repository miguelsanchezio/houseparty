About House Party

We started House Party after years and years of looking for party friendly places to rent. Bars, clubs, and raves can be really crazy sometimes. We wanted a place where we could invite all our friends and have a good time together, something more intimate. Without having to worry about being too loud or having to worry about abnoxious strangers.

Enjoy your stay at our great location in the Beverly Hills, CA. Every stay comes with a complimentary "Party Starter Pack" featuring premium drinks and snacks.
